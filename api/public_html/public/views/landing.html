Welcome to EasyBallot


The Ultimate Online Voting Guidance Tool

Vote Your Heart Out

This site is the best and easiest way to find out if your members of congress actually represent you when they vote on important bills. While you visit this site, you can pretend that YOU were elected to congress. Vote on as many of the same bills which congress's last session voted as you want. After each vote, get an instant update and find out whether your represenatives actually voted with you or against you. Easy Ballot automatically remembers your representatives and their Repworthiness. Repworthiness is a percentage calculated by comparing your votes to your representatives' votes. Easy Ballot works to help you keep things straight come November. It's easy. Even an opposing party member could use it.

Filters and Bill Search

Not everyone is directly affected by amendments to the Railroad Retirement Act of 1937. We have created filters which are chosen at sign up that will only display bills related to your areas of interest. Also, we've included a search bar so you can find and vote on bills like the Affordable Care Act, also known as Obamacare.
